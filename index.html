About me

Hi, I am Wilson, currently a Electrical and Computer Engineering master's student with a focus in robotics at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD). I've taken courses like sensing and estimation, motion planning, visual learning at UCSD, where I learned particle fileter/visual-inertial SLAM, Model-free prediction/control, Computer vision, etc.

Currently, I work as a software intern at Futurewei, where I work on ARVR platform. In my previous internship at FARobot, I was responsible for developing a LiDAR-based detection system for autonomous mobile robots. I also have research experience in deep learning (Computer Vision & Natural Language Processing & Multi-modal Learning) during my full-time/internship at CKIP Lab, Academia Sinica. Prior to my Master's, I completed my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering at National Taiwan University, where I've done projects like autonomous mobile robot for disinfection, autonomous propeller-powered vehicle.
